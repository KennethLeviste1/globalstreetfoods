About Us

Contest

This website was created to serve for two contests being the SkillsUSA contest and the 2024 Intuit WCSD CTE Department food truck website contest. At first, this website was intended to be only a food truck website until both of the Webmasters found out about the food truck contest in which they turned the website into the food truck website.

The Food Truck

Around mid 2023, Intuit and the WCSD CTE Department funded and bought a food truck. Students from Reed High school designed the theme which is a "Global" Theme. Next, students from Spanish Springs High School were able to design the food truck itself and the person who was able to design the food truck was named Ethan Gonzales.
